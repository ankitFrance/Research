A Deep Dive into Lazy Loading vs. Eager Loading

🌟 What is Lazy Loading?

Lazy loading defers loading non-essential resources until needed, improving performance.

⚡ What is Eager Loading?

Eager loading loads everything at once, reducing delays when accessing resources.
